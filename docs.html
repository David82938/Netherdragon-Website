Welcome to the Netherdragon Network documentation! Here you can find guides and resources to help you get the most out of your experience on our server. Whether you're a new player looking for tips or a veteran seeking detailed information about our features, this is the place to start.

Getting Started

Prerequisites

Before you can join the Netherdragon Network, make sure you have the following:

  • A Minecraft account (Java Edition)
  • The minecraft Launcher (preferrably the latest version)
  • A good internet connection

Once you have the prerequisites, you can look at the instructions on the homepage on how to connect to the server.

For new players

If you're new to the Netherdragon Network, here are some tips to get you started:

  • Explore the server and discover all the unique features we offer.
  • Join our community on Discord to connect with other players and stay updated on the latest news.
  • Read our rules and guidelines to ensure a positive experience for everyone.

Rules

The NetherDragon Network Team

How to join the team

You can ask for a position in the channel #team-applications of our Discord server.

Admins

Admins have the highest authority in the NetherDragon Network team. They can warn, kick, mute or ban players and have control over their assigned server.

Moderators

Moderators manage players and ensure a friendly atmosphere. They can warn, kick or mute players and can ban players under administrator consent.

Helpers

Helpers watch out for rule breakers and guide new players. They can report players to moderators and warn them.

Permission Abuse

A NetherDragon Network team member gets punished if he abuses their elevated permissions, for example by:

  • Banning players for no reason
  • Disrupting fair gameplay
  • etc...

The following actions will take part upon breaking the rules of a team member's current role:

  • They will get PERMANENTLY BANNED from the entire Network
  • They will get PERMANENTLY BANNED from the Discord Server
  • They will lose their server account data, including ranks, items and in-game money

These security measures exist, to protect the server from permission abuse and keep everything fair for everyone.
